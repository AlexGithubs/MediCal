What is Epic FHIR?

Epic FHIR allows this app to securely connect to your healthcare provider's system and automatically import your current medications, including dosages, timing, and special instructions from your doctor.

Step 1: Find Your Patient ID

Your Patient ID can be found in:

  • üì± Your Epic MyChart mobile app or website
  • üìÑ Hospital discharge papers or visit summaries
  • üí≥ Your hospital ID card or wristband
  • üìû Call your healthcare provider's office
Step 2: Enter Your Patient ID Safely
  • ‚úÖ Only enter YOUR OWN Patient ID
  • ‚úÖ Patient IDs usually look like: "ABC123-DEF456" or "12345678"
  • ‚úÖ Double-check for typos before saving
  • ‚ùå Never share your Patient ID with others
  • ‚ùå Don't enter someone else's Patient ID without permission
Step 3: Import Your Medications
  1. Go to Settings in the sidebar
  2. Check "Enable Epic FHIR integration"
  3. Enter your Patient ID in the text box
  4. Click "Save Settings"
  5. Click "Import Medications from Epic FHIR"
  6. Wait for the system to import your medications
üîí Your Data is Safe:
‚Ä¢ All data stays on your device - nothing is sent to external servers
‚Ä¢ Your Patient ID is validated and encrypted
‚Ä¢ The system creates automatic backups of your data

Troubleshooting

  • Patient ID not working? Double-check the format and contact your healthcare provider
